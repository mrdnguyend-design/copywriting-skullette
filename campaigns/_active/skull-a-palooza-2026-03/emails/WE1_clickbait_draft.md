### Joint Assessment

**Overall Score: ⭐⭐⭐⭐½**

**Top 3 Strengths:**

1. The subject line IS the hook — real customer language = highest open rate potential
2. Alice dialogue scene steals the email — funny, fast, believable
3. SMS CTA framed as access/necessity, not marketing opt-in — removes friction

**Top 3 Improvements:**

1. Add one sensory detail in the vault section (touch/weight of the bag)
2. P.S. could end warmer — "smallest apology" is fun but a tiny "welcome to the coven" beat would land better
3. Consider adding one more line after "nine dollars. and ninety-five cents." — let the number breathe before moving to "no catch"
