The first person to receive a SKULL-A-PALOOZA order is a woman named Maria.

Maria didn't register. Maria didn't set an alarm. Maria has no idea what SKULL-A-PALOOZA even is.

Let me explain.

Maria's husband is our USPS driver.

He came to pick up today's shipments. We've been packing SKULL-A-PALOOZA orders since 10am, and the warehouse looked like Christmas morning at a gothic post office. Boxes everywhere. Jamie's playlist at full volume. The whole scene.

He walked in, scanned the mountain of packages, and goes, "Busy day, huh."

Yes sir. SKULL-A-PALOOZA day.

Then he wandered over to the packing table where I had stock laid out. Picked up a Bloodrose wallet. Flipped it over. Ran his thumb across the skull detail on the clasp.

And went:

"...How much."

"$9.95."

Dead silence.

"You're lying."

"I'm not lying."

"THIS. Is $9.95??"

He put his scanner down on the counter. Stared at me for a solid three seconds. And said:

"I need to buy one for my wife."

So I sold our USPS driver a Bloodrose wallet. Right there at the packing station. Jamie was watching from across the room with his hand over his heart like he was witnessing a proposal.

His wife is apparently "obsessed with skulls and all that dark stuff" (his words, not mine). And when she sees what he paid for this wallet, she is going to... "lose her entire mind." His words again.

Which means Maria will receive her SKULL-A-PALOOZA order tonight. Hand-delivered by the same guy who shipped yours. 💀
