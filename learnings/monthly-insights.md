## Historical Analysis (May 2025 – Feb 2026)

*Based on 225 email campaigns analyzed from Klaviyo data.*

### Key Performance Patterns

**Open Rate Trend:**
- May 2025: 30.6% (small list, 477–4K recipients)
- Jun–Aug 2025: 25.6–27.8% (growing list, 20K–30K)
- Sep–Nov 2025: 21.2–22.4% (rapid list growth to 60K, diluted engagement)
- Dec 2025–Feb 2026: 24.6–26.3% (stabilizing, quality improving)

**Insight:** Open rates dipped during rapid list growth (Sep–Nov) then recovered. Current trajectory is positive.

### Best Performing Content Types

| Rank | Type | Evidence |
|------|------|----------|
| 1 | **Weather/Event + Julie reaction** | Snow Storm = 39.8% open (all-time best) |
| 2 | **Survey/Interactive** | Bag Charm Survey = 31.9% open + 1.5% click (best click) |
| 3 | **Industry takedown** | Fast Fashion = 30.5% open, Scamming = 31.7% |
| 4 | **Product launches** | La Roja = 33.8% open (small list) |
| 5 | **Customer stories** | Weekend Specials Day 1 = 32.7% open |

### Worst Performing Patterns

| Pattern | Evidence | Fix |
|---------|----------|-----|
| **Long teaser sequences (5+)** | BFBF teasers 2-4: all ~19.7% open | Cap teasers at 3-4, vary angles |
| **SMS recruitment emails** | "Why SMS" = 19.6% | Don't use email to sell SMS signup |
| **Post-campaign followups** | "Failed Saleperson" = 19.9% | Skip self-referential post-mortems |

### Story Source Performance

| Source | Avg Open Rate | Recommendation |
|--------|---------------|---------------|
| Basic Brenda Encounter | 26.7% | **Increase** — under-used (only 8/225) |
| Industry Commentary | 30.5% | **Increase** — only 1 email, huge potential |
| Product Origin | 25.2% | **Maintain** — solid performer |
| Witch Victory | 24.9% | **Maintain** — customer stories always work |
| Gothic Mundane | 24.1% | **Reduce** — overused at 64/225, needs variety |
| Cultural Darkness | 24.2% | **Reduce** — overused at 78/225, diluting impact |
| Julie Personal | 23.1% | **Develop** — only 3 emails, needs more entries |

### Hook Type Performance

| Hook | Avg Open Rate | Note |
|------|---------------|------|
| mystery_statement | 27.2% | Best performer — "confession", "don't tell" |
| quote_open | 26.3% | Customer quotes as openers work well |
| question | 24.6% | Reliable, high volume (62 emails) |
| scene_setting | 24.1% | Default/safe — most common (91 emails) |
| direct_offer | 23.9% | Lowest — sale emails suffer when too direct |
| urgency | 22.6% | Worst — pure urgency doesn't fit Julie's voice |

### Character Usage Insights

- **Alice + Jade** appearances correlate with 27.4% avg open rate (vs 24.6% overall) → **Use more often**
- **Basic Brenda** only appears in 3 emails but at 25.8% → **Under-utilized asset**
- **The Witch** (customer) appears in 77/225 but performs at 25.2% → slightly above average, keep steady

### Campaign Series Analysis

- **Weekend Specials:** 27.2% open rate — best series format
- **Friday 13th:** 26.2% — strong thematic alignment with brand
- **Small Bag Clearance:** 24.7% — solid
- **Final 300:** 23.3% — gamified format underperforming expectations
- **Mothers Day:** 24.4% — room for improvement with Julie Personal angle
