# /write-email — Infotainment Copywriter (7-Phase Core)

> **The main workflow.** Viết email Infotainment từ A → Z.
> **Email types:** daily (daily_), sale (sale_), teaser (teaser_)

---

## Phase 1: 📋 BRIEF (User Input)

Nhận brief từ user. Hỏi nếu thiếu:

- **Email type:** Daily / Sale / Teaser?
- **Sản phẩm/Broccoli:** Bag nào cần feature? Offer gì? (Ví dụ: "Lunacat BOGO", "Moonreaver + mystery bag", "Legacy Collection 50% off")
- **Context/Hot Dog hint:** Có story seed nào không? Customer email? Seasonal tie-in? Mundane moment?
- **Images:** Có hình ảnh cần embed không?
- **Campaign:** Email này thuộc series nào? (standalone / day X of sale / teaser cho upcoming event)
- **Tone đặc biệt:** Celebratory? Urgent? Reflective? Playful? Chaotic?

### For Sale Emails, thêm:
- **Offer details:** Discount %, tiers, freebies, bundles
- **Scarcity info:** Quantity limits, time limits, tier thresholds
- **Deadline:** Khi nào kết thúc?

## Phase 4: ✍️ DRAFT (Write Complete Email)

### Cấu trúc email:

#### 1. Subject Line
- Short, curiosity-driven, Julie's voice
- Dark/weird/personal > generic promotional

#### 2. Preview Text
- 1 line bổ sung context (KHÔNG lặp subject line)

#### 3. The Hook (Gothic Seinfeld Opening)
- Bắt đầu giữa hành động. Sensory details. Inner monologue.
- ❌ KHÔNG bao giờ bắt đầu bằng "Hey witch!" / "I hope you are well" / "Happy [holiday]!"
- ✅ Bắt đầu bằng: dialogue, action, hoặc 1 khoảnh khắc cụ thể
- Sensory details:
  - *Smell:* old books, rain, extinguished candles, leather, coffee
  - *Sound:* combat boots on pavement, thunder, cat knocking things over
  - *Internal:* (I really shouldn't buy another skull... okay, maybe just one)

#### 4. The Narrative Arc
- Kể chuyện. Relatable. Vivid imagery.
- Include dialogue where possible (actual conversations, not summaries)
- Show, don't tell: "Her face went through three emotions in half a second" > "She was surprised"
- CAPSLOCK for drama. (Parentheses for inner thoughts.)
- 5th-grade reading level. Punchy. Lots of spacing.

#### 5. The Pivot (Bridge)
- Kết nối story → Big Idea → Product — naturally
- Can be slightly jarring (Ben Settle's rule: "if it feels too smooth, you smoothed out the selling")
- Classic bridges:
  - "It made me realize something..."
  - "And that's exactly why I designed..."
  - "Here's the thing."

#### 6. The Sales Pitch (Broccoli wrapped in Hot Dog)
- Giới thiệu product như weapon/treasure, không phải spec sheet
- "The Raven Tote isn't just a bag. It's a mood. With zippers that bite back."
- Wrap features: "wine-dark lining hiding all her little mysteries" > "red interior lining"

#### For Sale Emails specifically:
- Offer in **FIRST THIRD** of email (don't bury the deal)
- Tiered scarcity with specific numbers (not vague "limited stock")
- FOMO future-pacing: "You'll be emailing me Monday like: 'Do you have just one more?'"
- Multiple CTAs throughout (not just at the end)
- NO apologizing for selling (GAMESHOW principle)

#### 7. The CTA
- Direct command: "Go scare the neighbors," "Claim your darkness," "Wield this"
- Link placeholder: [LINK] or "Shop [Bag Name] Here"

#### 8. Sign Off
- "Stay spooky," / "Stay wicked," / "Stay weird," / "Stay in control,"
- Julie, Founder, Skullette.com

#### 9. P.S.
- Witty comment, scarcity reminder, future tease, or hater takedown
- Optional P.P.S. for personal touch (addressing specific customer mentioned in story)

### Logic Check (chạy trước khi present draft):
- [ ] Character consistency — Julie/Alice/Jade/customers in-character?
- [ ] Timeline logic — Timestamps/events make sense?
- [ ] Emotional arc — vulnerability → revelation → pride → action?
- [ ] Scene believability — Tình huống có thể xảy ra ngoài đời?
- [ ] Voice check — Nghe như witchy best friend texting at 2 AM, hay press release?
- [ ] Offer check (sale only) — Offer in first third? Clear scarcity? Multiple CTAs?

## Phase 6: 📨 FINALIZE (Platform-Ready Output)

### Step 1: Output Plain Text (Markdown)
- Clean final draft cho review/archive
- Include: Subject Line, Preview Text, Full Email Body, Sign-off, P.S.

### Step 2: Generate HTML Email

// turbo
Read `knowledge/templates/email-html-template.md` cho format reference.

Convert plain text draft → HTML theo format sau:

#### Mỗi paragraph → wrap trong:
```html
<p><span style="font-size: 16px; font-family: Helvetica, Arial, sans-serif; font-weight: 400;">
  Content here
</span></p>
```

#### Bold — Thêm `<strong>` cho câu cần nhấn mạnh:
```html
<p><span style="font-size: 16px; font-family: Helvetica, Arial, sans-serif; font-weight: 400;">
  <strong>They're GONE. EXTINCT. FOREVER.</strong>
</span></p>
```

#### Rules:
- **Mỗi dòng ngắn** (Julie's punchy style) → là 1 `<p><span>` riêng
- **CAPSLOCK sentences** → thêm `<strong>` bọc ngoài
- **CTA lines** → thêm `<strong>` cho nổi bật
- **Parenthetical asides** → giữ nguyên trong `<span>`, KHÔNG italic
- **Line breaks** giữa sections → dùng `<br>` hoặc `<p>` trống
- **Links** → `<a href="{{URL}}">Link Text</a>` trong `<span>`

### Step 3: Save HTML File
Save HTML output as file: `knowledge/brand/examples/[type]_[tên_ngắn].html`
- File phải chứa complete HTML document (DOCTYPE, head, body)
- Ready to paste trực tiếp vào email platform (Klaviyo/Mailchimp)

### Step 4: Subject Line Options
Generate **5 subject line options:**
- Curiosity/mystery
- Negative/warning
- Story-driven
- Weird/dark
- Direct benefit (for sale emails)

## Phase 7: 📁 ARCHIVE (Save Final Draft)

Save final draft to `knowledge/brand/examples/`:

Naming convention:
```
[type]_[tên_ngắn].md     → Plain text
[type]_[tên_ngắn].html   → HTML ready
```

| Prefix | Loại | Đặc điểm |
|--------|------|----------|
| `daily_` | Daily Email | Story-driven, có link về store |
| `teaser_` | Teaser Email | Không chứa link store (link survey, đăng ký...) |
| `sale_` | Sale Email | Chứa sale event, urgency, tiered scarcity |

## Phase 8: 🔄 KAIZEN (Continuous Improvement)

Sau khi email được duyệt và archive, ghi nhận bài học vào `knowledge/kaizen-log.md`:

### Step 1: Log Entry
Thêm entry mới:
```markdown
### Entry #[XXX] — [YYYY-MM-DD]
**Email:** [Tên email]
**Type:** [Daily / Sale / Teaser]
**Product:** [Product/Offer]
**Rating:** ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ (1-5)
```

### Step 2: What Worked (KEEP DOING)
Ghi 3-5 điểm mạnh cụ thể:
- Technique nào tạo impact mạnh nhất?
- Scene/line nào vivid nhất?
- CTA structure nào effective?
- Emotional arc có smooth không?

### Step 3: What Needed Revision (AVOID NEXT TIME)
Ghi lại corrections đã thực hiện trong quá trình viết:
- Revisions nào user yêu cầu?
- Ben/Daniel critique nào đúng nhất?
- Pattern lỗi nào đang lặp lại?

### Step 4: Update Pattern Tracker
Cập nhật bảng ở đầu kaizen-log:
- Laws nào được dùng? Hiệu quả sao?
- Mechanisms nào được dùng?
- Hook style nào?

### Step 5: Flag Workflow Updates (nếu có)
Nếu phát hiện improvement → thêm vào section "Workflow Updates Pending" trong kaizen-log. Sẽ được review + apply mỗi 10 entries.
