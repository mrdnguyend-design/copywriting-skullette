Oh no. This bag was a mistake.
Okay, I need to confess something.
I think I made a mistake.
Not a “oops wrong stitching” kind of mistake.
(Those are easy to fix.)
No… this is worse.
This is a what if nobody likes it kind of mistake.
See, a few days ago I was bored (and needed a break from my daughter 😂)…
So I crashed at my bestie’s place.
She’s like me—into skulls, black boots, and everything bold.
But that day?
I blinked. “Faith over Fear”?
Then I blurted out:
“Wait… you bought THAT?”
She just shrugged.
“Don’t you think it’s… motivating?”
And honestly? Yeah. It was.
It didn’t scream. It didn’t growl.
It was quiet. Calm.
But it hit like a punch to the chest.
And then my brain did what it always does:
“What if this… but black leather?”
That’s how this bag was born.
But here’s the problem:
It’s nothing like what I usually make.
No skull. No roses.
Just black leather.
And across the front—just one line:
“Shake thyself from the dust & arise.”
A message that hits deeper than fashion.
And the voice in my head immediately went:
“Oh Julie… this is too different. Nobody will buy it.”
But another voice said:
“Faith over fear, remember?”
So I made it anyway.
And now here I am… sending it to you with slightly sweaty hands.
If you love it?
Please tell me.

And if this one speaks to you...

Because honestly? I wasn’t sure if anyone else would want something this different.
So… what do you think?
With a nervous heart (and a little hope),
