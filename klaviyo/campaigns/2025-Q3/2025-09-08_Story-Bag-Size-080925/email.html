My INBOX (Sunday, 3:04 PM):
✉️ “Will the wallet fit?”
✉️ “Will the bag fit my planner?”
✉️ “What about my Bible?”
✉️ “Water bottle?”
✉️ “Lunch?”
✉️ “My CAT??”

ME (headphones on, cold coffee in hand):
...What did I start?

Every time I post a new bag — like Raven Skull, 3 days ago —
you fall in love at first scroll…

…and then my inbox goes full chaos.

But I get it.

Because you’re not just buying a look.
You’re buying something to carry your life.

And your life?
Yeah. She’s got layers.

So let’s settle this once and for all.

Let’s play a game I just invented:
“Does It Fit?”

💀 ROUND ONE: “THE ESSENTIALS”

  • ✅ A 13” laptop
  • ✅ Your planner or notebook
  • ✅ A Bible (yes, we actually checked)
  • ✅ The FREE matching wallet (that comes with every bag)
  • ✅ Snacks. Sunglasses. Lipstick army. Keys. Headphones. Charger. Sanity.

💀 ROUND TWO: “THE CHAOS PACK”

  • ✅ Three receipts from 2019
  • ✅ Four painkillers and a granola bar
  • ✅ Your daily tarot draw
  • ✅ Your future therapist’s business card
  • ✅ Everything you swore you’d “clean out later”

💀 FINAL ROUND: “THE NOPE ZONE”

  • ❌ A desktop PC
  • ❌ A whole cat
  • ❌ Your ex’s excuses

Let’s be real:
You carry a lot.

Not just things.
Roles.
Expectations.
An entire microcosm of "what ifs" and "just in cases" zipped into one bag.

And this leather?
She’s built for it.

Some days, you're minimalist.
Phone. Wallet. Lip gloss.
Use the thin, elegant strap. Light. Effortless. Witchy chic.

Other days?
You’re a walking preparedness kit.
You need the chunky leather strap.
Wide. Strong. Built to carry weight without hurting your shoulder’s feelings.

And hey—if it arrives and somehow isn’t your vibe?
You've got 30 days to return it.
No drama. No rituals.
Just pack it back up and send it home.

So...

If this bag sounds like it fits your life?
It probably does.

And yes —
it still comes with a FREE matching wallet
(Because you deserve beautiful things that actually work.)

 
Pick your favorite and bring your whole world with you
