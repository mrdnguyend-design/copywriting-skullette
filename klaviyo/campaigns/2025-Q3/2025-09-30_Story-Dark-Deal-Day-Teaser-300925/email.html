POP.
Another email lands in my inbox.

Big. Polished.  Gold serif logo.
Subject line: “You’re invited.”

Okay…
I wasn’t going to say anything.

But this?
I had to tell you.

Because that email — Wasn’t just a launch.
It was an invitation.

To us.

I know. Shocking, right?

You know how small we are...
No billboards.
No celebrity stylists.
No glittery pop-ups in overpriced malls.

And yet…
Someone
somewhere
looked at us and said:

“Yes. These skull bags. These are the ones.”

And now…
We’re in.

A curated event for rising indie brands.
And somehow… they picked Skullette.
(Still not sure if it was the skulls or the leather. Either way — we’re in.)

It’s a real event. Real brands. Real booths.
But here’s what makes it different —
You don’t have to show up.
You don’t even have to leave your couch.
We’re opening the same deal online, just for you.

The organizers asked us to design a game.
Something dark.
Mysterious.
Interactive.

Rachel built a spinning wheel that glows red and hisses.
(I wish I was joking. It growls.)

We were just about to pack it up…

Then I stopped.
Wait.

We’ve never let you play.

You, who’s been here since the beginning.
You, who treats your bag like a weapon and a ritual.
You, who already knows leather isn’t just a look —
It’s a feeling.

Why should strangers get the first spin?

So…
I’m letting you in.
Before the event opens.
Before the lights go up.

And I’m calling it:

🖤 DARK DEAL DAY 🖤

One day. One bag. One dare.

Here’s how it works:
👜 You buy a leather bag.
🎁 I choose the design.
🤫 You won’t know which one until it arrives.

Could be Bloodrose.
Could be Dustveil.
Could be something no one’s seen yet.

But whatever you get…
It came from the vault.

Get a full-size Skullette bag for $79.95
(Normally $129.95)
Only on Dark Deal Day — Friday, Oct 3

The event organizers wanted a “special rate.”
But I took it further.

If we’re going to make a scene…
Let’s make it count.

So I priced it almost to break-even.
Not to make money.
But to make a statement.

And yes — you actually pay $79.95.
No tricks. No “maybe” discount.
It’s a full-size Skullette leather bag — for $50 off.
But only on October 3rd.

“JULIE. You’re giving away the brand.”
(– My accountant, kicking down the door.)

She capped us at 100 bags total.
We split them:
45 for the event.
55 for you.

That’s more bags for our email crew than the entire luxury crowd.
Because let’s be honest:
You’re the reason we’re even going.

But only if you get there first.
(And you know what happens to our vault drops…)

📅 Friday, October 3rd at 10 AM ET

Set your alarm. Charge your phone. And if your luck is wicked enough...
You might just win what’s inside.

P.S.
Is there a bag you secretly hope is in the vault?
Reply and tell me.

No promises…
But I do have sticky fingers.
And a soft spot for those who ask.

🖤
Julie
Founder, Skullette.com
