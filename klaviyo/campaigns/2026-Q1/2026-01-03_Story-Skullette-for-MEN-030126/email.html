I'm sitting at my desk this morning.

Black coffee. Stormy weather playlist on repeat. Inbox full of the usual chaos.

(You know. The normal Tuesday vibes.)

I'm about to delete a spam email trying to sell me "beach-ready summer sandals" when—

Wait.

What's THIS?

An email. From a MAN. Named Roger.

And Roger… Roger just said something that made me stop mid-sip and actually SMILE.

"Hi, seen your bags for little while and when i know my wife 'doesnt need' another pocketbook i will be getting her one regardless."

HOLD ON.

Did this man just say his wife doesn't NEED another bag… so he's buying her one ANYWAY?

(King behavior. Absolute king behavior. 👑)

He continues:

"I love your skulls on everything theme. May i suggest with your bigger scraps make stuff that matches for guys? I know im in the minority here but your prints and patterns are very eye catching."

I had to read this twice.

Birthdays. Anniversaries. The classic "I messed up and need to fix this NOW" emergency purchase.

But THIS?

"Okay cool… but what if I wanted skulls TOO?"

Same dark leather. Same gothic buckles. Same "I'm-not-here-to-make-friends" energy.

Just… made for men.

And honestly?

I'm intrigued.

Black. Brown. Navy. Maybe gray if they're feeling "edgy."

What about the men who married US?

(They clearly have good taste. Just saying.)

So before I run into my workshop swinging leather like a woman possessed… I need to do this PROPERLY.

You.

My witches.
