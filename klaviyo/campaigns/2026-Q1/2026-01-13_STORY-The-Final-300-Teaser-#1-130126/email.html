The office door SLAMMED open.

(I almost spilled my coffee. Again.)

Lucy rushed in, waving her phone like she’d just unlocked a new level in a game.

“685 orders, Julie! We’re so close to 700! Isn’t this AMAZING?!”

She stood there. Smiling. Waiting for a celebration.

Instead?

“I’m in trouble.”

Lucy froze.

“…What? We’re doing great. How is this bad?”

I looked up at her and sighed.

“Because I didn’t bet on 700, Lucy.”

“I bet on 1,000. For sure, not with you.”

Let me rewind.

Last month, I was at the tannery.

It smells like old leather, dust, and questionable life choices.

(Weirdly comforting. Like a goth library.)

I’m touching this roll of deep, midnight-black leather—smooth, heavy, dangerous—when Madame Rossi walks over.

She’s in her 60s.

Silver hair pulled back tight.

Runs the best leather supply operation around.

She’s been doing this for 40 years.

She’s intimidating.

And I’m convinced she can smell fear.

She looks at my order sheet. Raises an eyebrow. Then laughs.

Not a cute laugh.

The kind of laugh that says, “Oh sweetheart… nooooo.”

“You’re a small brand. This design is bold. You’ll sell 700 bags. Maybe. Don’t get carried away.”

Did my ego flare up?

(Yes. Immediately. Bad habit. I’m not fixing it.)

“I can sell 1,000,”

Her eyes lit up. Uh-oh.

“Alright,”

“Let’s make it interesting.”

“If you sell 1,000 in the first month, I give you 10% off your next bulk order.”

That alone is huge. Suppliers do not do discounts like that.

“And… I give you access to my Midnight Batch leather.”

I almost stopped breathing.

Midnight Batch is leather you cannot buy anywhere else.

She makes it once a year.

And she’s never offered it outside her inner circle.

“Deal,”

I said, trying to stay cool.

“And if you lose?”

She smiled. A dangerous smile.

“Then you buy steak dinner for my entire factory floor.”

“All 50 of us.”

…

FIFTY. STEAKS.

Do you know how much that costs?!

But I said yes. The deal is good. And I love this challenge.

Fast-forward to now.

I’m staring at the dashboard.

685 sales.

If we stop at 700, Madame Rossi wins.

I lose the discount.

I lose the Midnight Batch leather.

And I lose a horrifying amount of money buying steak for 50 very smug Italian leather workers.

(She’ll probably make me wear an apron that says LOSER.)

Absolutely NOOOO.

“We’re not stopping at 700.”

“We’re hitting 1,000.”

“And yes,” I added,

“I’m going to do anything to reach it.”

And here’s the truth.

I can’t buy the last 300 bags myself.

(The accountant would pass out. Also… that’s sad.)

But I know who can help me flip this.

YOU.

The witches who’ve been here from the start.

The ones who don’t need Skullette explained or softened.

So I’m offering a very shameless bribe.

Win-win situation:

You help me beat Madame Rossi (and save me from financial ruin).

I sneak you into a SECRET SALE that’s only for the pre-registered list.

No public links. No chaos. No fighting over the last Corvexia.

Just a quiet back door for people who get it.

(Nothing personal, Madame Rossi. Okay. It’s personal.)

If you want a Corvexia bag or literally anything else at a price that will make my accountant cry…

Text FINAL to 1-866-580-9334

(Toll-free. No spam. Just wicked deals.)

No forms. No hoops.

Just sneak in before everyone else notices.

Help me win this.

—Julie

Founder, Skullette 🖤
