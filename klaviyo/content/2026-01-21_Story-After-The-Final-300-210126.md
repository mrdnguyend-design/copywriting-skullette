## Body
I'm staring at my laptop at 17:47 PM yesterday.

Refreshing. Refreshing. Refreshing.

The number sits at998.

Two more bags. That's all I need to hit 1,000 and win this stupid bet with Madame Rossi.

(Who, by the way, has been sending me increasingly snippy emails all week about how "American women always quit before the finish line." RUDE.)

I refresh again.999

ONE MORE.

I'm sitting there in my black silk robe(obviously), watching this screen like it owes me money, when—

DING.

Order #1,000.

From someone in Oregon who bought The Raven Tote.

I scream. Loudly.(My husband thinks I've finally lost it.)

But I don't care because I WON.

1,000 bags in less than a month.10% off my next leather order. Access to the premium Italian hides I've been dreaming about since 2022.

And most importantly?

The satisfaction of watching Madame Rossi eat her words.

This morning, I sent her a very professional email that said:"We hit 1,000. I'll take that discount now, thanks."

Her response?

"Congratulations. The leather will be ready in February."

(No apology. No "I was wrong." Just Italian stubbornness wrapped in three sentences. Whatever. I'll take it.)

So here I am this morning, riding high on victory and espresso, dreaming about what I'll make with that luxury leather.

New shapes? Maybe.

New colors? Always.

But then I opened my inbox and everything changed.

Most of the messages were the usual chaos:

"Where's my discount code?!"

(There wasn't one. That was the point.)

"I already have too many Skullette bags!"

(That's not a problem. That's a COLLECTION.)

But then I saw this one. From Kristina Lozon-Kimling:

"I have more handbags in my collection than I could possibly use in three lifetimes. Have you considered creating bag charms or keyfinders to complement your bags? Those I can use."

I stopped. Read it again.

And thought: Holy hell. She's right.

You witches have been buying these bags like they're going out of style (they're not).

Some of you have five. Some have ten. One of you has SEVENTEEN.(You know who you are.)

But charms?

Skull charms. Leather tassels. Little gothic trinkets you can clip onto your bag?

That's something you can NEVER have too many of.

So here's what I'm doing.

I want YOU to design the first Skullette Charm Collection.

Send me photos of charms you love. Skull ones. Spooky ones. Sassy ones. Leather, metal, whatever. If it screams "Skullette," I want to see it.

And if your idea is good enough?

When that premium Italian leather shows up in February, we're making YOUR charm.

You'll get credit. You'll get the first one free. And you'll go down in Skullette history as the witch who started the charm revolution.

Please, reply this email with any photo of your favorite charm

Oh, and one more thing.

If you missed the Final 300 madness last week?

You can still get this:Buy ANY tote or handbag → Get a FREE mystery bag.

Not as wild as the 35% off insanity. But still good.

Now go forth. Send me charm ideas. Let's build the next thing together.

—Julie

P.S. Kristina, if you're reading this: You just started something. I hope you're ready for the chaos.
