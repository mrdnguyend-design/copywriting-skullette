New year, new problem… and nearly 200 bags gone

## Body
Okay. You know how people say“new year, new me”?

Yeah… well… my warehouse decided to go with:“new year, new PROBLEM.”

Because yesterday — literally the first day of the year — I walked in to do my little warehouse check.

Section A? Good.

Section B? Still good.

Section C? I’m walking in all cheerful, thinking“wow, fresh start!”…

And then I see it:A quiet little ceiling leak dripping onto the floor.

It wasn’t a disaster but I still froze for a moment, just trying to process what I was looking at.

Then reality hit, and I ran across the room like someone’s grandma chasing kids with a slipper, checking every stack of leather as carefully as humanly possible.

Good news:none of the bags are damaged. 🙏

Bad news:I swear I aged 12 years instantly.

And now… here’s where things get tricky:

I cannot keep those bags in Section C.

That tiny leak might look harmless now, but one heavy rain and I could lose every single one of them.

Yesterday I managed a temporary fix — emphasis on temporary.

But these bags need to move immediately.

The problem?

Sections A & B are already full…which means moving Section C requires a whole new warehouse:new rent, new setup, new logistics, new everything.

And since I literally just paid for a huge batch of leather… there is absolutely no budget left to pull that off.

I turned to Linda — the warehouse manager of that cursed corner — and she goes:

“Oh yeah… we have about 200 small bags in that section. And honestly? They’re selling kinda slow.”

Ma’am. About. Two. Hundred.

The business isn’t dying, but my HEART definitely did a tiny scream.

Okay. Fine. It seems my witches really love the big bags more than the small ones.

So I stared at that mountain of tiny bags… did the dramatic founder sigh… and made a decision:

We’re clearing every single one of them.

Partly because I need to move them somewhere safe…

And partly because I need the money to fix that ceiling properly before it decides to create“round two”.

Tomorrow. 10 A.M. Eastern.

60% OFF.

Around 200 small leather bags

Today, the team is checking every item — quantity, stitching, zippers, quality — the whole thing.

And the other important reason I’m telling you this today, not tomorrow?

So you actually have time to prepare. Because you know…

Tomorrow? The REAL new-year chaos begins.

If you like traveling light or want a small bag for the new year or don’t enjoy carrying something the size of a toddler…

Tomorrow is YOUR moment.

But remember:

We only have about 200.

I am NOT restocking these.

And they go live at exactly 10:00 A.M. Eastern.

Set an alarm for 9:55 AM.

Set two alarms if your phone likes to betray you.

Trust me — you’ll want to be ready.

The ceiling has spoken.

And tomorrow… we deal with it.

—Julie

Founder, Skullette 🖤
