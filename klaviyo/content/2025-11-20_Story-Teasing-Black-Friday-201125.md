## Body
Hey ,

I need your help.Urgent help.

I was supposed to sit down today, act like a responsible founder,and plan a normal Black Friday sale.

You know — pick a discount, write a sentence, call it a day. Simple. Safe. Predictable.

Instead?My brain decided violence.

It said:

“Too trivial, Julie.

Ask your Dark Queens what the craziest idea is…

and then actually do it.”

So here we are.

Black Friday at Skullette runs Nov 27 – Dec 1

That’s the only part that’s settled.

Everything else?Up to you.

Because you’re the one carrying Skullette into your everyday life — office doors, brunch tables, grocery aisles, church pews(still iconic).

You’re the one who turns skulls into style.

You’re the one strangers whisper about.

So if this sale is going off the rails?

So tell me, my witch…what kind of chaos are we starting?

OPTION A: The Lucky Refund Madness

Imagine checking out like normal.

Order placed. Tracking number in your inbox. Everything routine.

And then suddenly you get:

“Your entire order has been refunded. You keep everything.”

Partner:“What did you buy?”

You:“Talk to Julie.”

Chaotic? Yes. Legendary? Alsoyes.

OPTION B: The Hidden Code Hunt

Somewhere in my Black Friday emails…

A code will be hidden. Not highlighted. Not obvious. Not “click-me” bright.

Think:one tiny detail that doesn’t look right until you stare too long. A letter out of place. A line that feels suspicious.

First women to find it getthe biggest discount of the entire sale.

OPTION C: The Mystery Gift Drop

At checkout you get a prompt:

“Add a mystery gift?”

No preview. No clues. Just pure risk vs. reward.

Then on delivery day, you open it and either whisper“no way…”or scream loud enough for the neighbors to judge you.

Either outcome? A moment worth having.

OPTION D: The ‘Julie, Sit Down’ Idea

Maybe your brain has something even wilder:

✅A reward for the highest cart.

✅A one-hour flash event.

✅Five random women chosen for 100% free orders.

Or something I haven’t even thought of yet.

If your idea scares me a little? Good.

HERE'S WHAT I NEED"

I’m reading every reply.

And yes —I’ll choose the final Black Friday planbased on what you tell me.

This isn’t pretend involvement. It’s your sale as much as mine.

And while we’re plotting chaos…
