## Body
I'm at my desk this morning, second cup of coffee(black, obviously), scrolling through emails when I see this:

"I've been following your brand for a long time and bought a Nyxrose bag. I really like the design, but have you ever thought about designingEmbossed Goth Style bags? I think these will help you reach more customers.

Sandra"

I stop scrolling.

Read it again.

And I think... WHO IS THIS WOMAN?

Is she a leather expert? A business consultant? Someone who knows something I don't?

Or just another customer with opinions?

(Spoiler: She's cooler than I thought.)

Because here's the thing — Sandra didn't just buy a bag and disappear like most people.

She bought a bag... THEN came back to tell me where the brand should go next.

That's not annoying.

That's loyalty.

So I screenshot the email and send it to Jade(our designer).

Me:"What do you think?"

Jade:"Embossed could work... but we'd need contrast. Otherwise it's just black on black and looks flat."

Me:"So we add something."

Jade:"Like what?"

Me:"I don't know. YOU'RE the designer."

Three days later,Jade sent me 4 concept designs.

Things we've NEVER made before.

And I'm staring at them thinking...

One of these is going to piss off the play-it-safe crowd.

PERFECT.

Here's the deal:

I'm not telling you what these designs are yet.

(Where's the fun in that?)

But I need to know which ones YOU would actually pick.

Not which ones look"nice."

Not which ones are"interesting."

Which ones make you go"SHUT UP AND TAKE MY MONEY."

Because I only make bags that people actually want.

Bags that announce who you are the second you walk in the room.

Not bags that blend into the beige void of Target totes and canvas nonsense.

So I made a 1-minute survey with all 4 designs.

Click here and tell me which ones your dark soul craves

But here's the catch:

This survey closes Sunday at midnight.

After that, I'm picking the TOP 2 designs based on what you tell me.

And those are the ONLY two we're making.

The other two? Gone forever into the design graveyard.

So if you have a favorite... you better vote for it.

And if you have OTHER chaotic ideas(coffin-shaped clutches? Secret tarot pockets? Lining the color of dried blood?),

Write me.Let me know. Share your imagination.

I'll read every word while drinking wine.

(And probably judging you a little. But in a fun way.)

Take the survey before Sunday midnight

The coven decides what we make next.

So speak up.

Stay spooky,

Julie

P.S.If you're the type who thinks "embossed goth bags are too much," this survey is NOT for you. Go find a beige tote at Target. We'll be over here making things that actually have a personality.
