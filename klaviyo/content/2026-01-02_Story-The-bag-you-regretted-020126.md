## Body
Late last night — like should’ve-been-asleep late — Jade from my creative team drops a link into the Skullette group chat and goes:“Uh… should we be worried?”

(It is NEVER good when someone opens with that.)

The link?

A Reddit thread titled: “Show me the bag you regretted buying.”

Instant chaos in my group.

“Oh WOW… that bag is so tiny it can’t even fit her phone. Yeah, okay, I’d regret that too.”

“Too heavy, even when it’s empty? What is this? a brick sewn inside for emotional support?”

“And look. Coach. YSL. LV. Over and over again.”

“Yeah… people paid a lot of money. Of course they’re mad. Expectations were sky-high.”

So I kept scrolling. And scrolling.

And finally…“…okay. I don’t see Skullette.”

No dramatic Reddit roast.

No “worst purchase of my life” confession featuring our bags.

Lucky us because we didn’t get dragged there 😮‍💨

Honestly, reading one negative review would probably put me in a bad mood for the entire first month of the new year.

But if you’re thinking,“So… are you afraid of bad reviews?”

Honestly? Yeah, obvious. This is any brand owner’s fear 😅

Not because we can’t handle criticism —but because criticism is how we’ve always improved.

The very first bags I ever made?

They were heavily criticized.

So I redesigned. Scrapped ideas.Started again.

And that’s how we got to the versions of Skullette bags you see today.

But even now? You think they’re good?

Noo… We still get 1-star reviews 😂

Like the leather“wasn’t the best quality in the world.”

And honestly? They weren’t wrong.

We’re not a billion-dollar fashion house.

We’re a small, skull-obsessed brand with small capital and a big attitude.

So we make choices.

We choosegood leather for the price.

We choosestrong prints that don’t flake or fade.

We choosebags you can actually USE.

That’s why our handbags and totes are roomy.

That’s why even our mini satchels and circle bags still fit your phone, keys, and makeup (yes — even the dramatic eyeliner).

So if you’re starting the new year thinking:“I just want ONE bag I won’t side-eye six months from now, Julie”

This is me — and honestly, a whole coven of witches — gently but confidently pointing at

Skullette’s Signature Collection

Our Best Gothic Leather Bags In The World

They might not be perfect all the time.

But surely they’re made to be used, not abandoned at the back of your closet with regret.

And yes —the winter sale is still very much alive.

Grab one.

Start the new year without buyer’s remorse 😌

Stay dark, stay practical,

Julie

Founder, Skullette.com
