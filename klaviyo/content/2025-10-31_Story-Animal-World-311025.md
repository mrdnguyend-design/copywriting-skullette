I need to confess something.

You’ve broken the design team.

“I NEED a bat design!!”

“Where’s the Raven bag???”

“Omg PLEASE bring back the black cat one!”

Yeah. I heard you. I read every single request.

And my mistake?

Ever since that day?

Every time I check their sketch board…

There’s a new animal.

A fox.

A raven.

A black cat.

A snake.

A fanged rabbit (?!).

And I said:

“WHAT IS HAPPENING??”

They just looked at me. Wide-eyed. Innocent.

“You said they loved animals,”they whispered.

“You said they wanted more bats. More cats. More ravens.”

(Which is technically true. But I didn’t mean this much enthusiasm.)

It’s like we accidentally launched Skullette:Animal Kingdom Edition.

And they’re not just doodling.

They’re testing styles now.

Sketch. Ink. Watercolor. Digital painting.

Yesterday, I walked in and someone was drawing…a black cat with wings.

So yeah. I’m excited.

I’m genuinely confused.
