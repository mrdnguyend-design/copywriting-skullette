# Skullette SMS — Template System
> 10 rotating angles for SMS campaigns.
> Read before `/write-sms`. Cross-ref with `content-taxonomy.md` Part 2B.

---

## GOLDEN RULES

1. **Never say "VIP" or "EXCLUSIVE"** — show it through behavior, not labels
2. **One joke/angle per SMS** — don't stack. Pick one, commit.
3. **Lead with relationship, not deal** — the deal is the excuse, not the reason
4. **Keep under 320 chars core message** (before link + sign-off)
5. **Rotate angles** — never same angle twice in one week
6. **Product name + price anchor always** — specific, not vague
7. **One character max per SMS** — Julie is default. Others are devices.
8. **End with warmth** — "— Julie" or "Stay spooky, Julie 🖤" or "— J"

## 10 ROTATING ANGLES

### Angle 1: "I Hid One For You"
**Tone:** Warm, loyal, coven-sister energy
**When:** Premium products, small quantity deals, loyalty moments
**Template:**
```
Hey [name],
I set one aside before we posted it.
[Product] — $[price] (was $[original]).
Not announced yet. Just for the coven.
[link]
— Julie 🖤
```
**Product fit:** Handbags, limited editions, bundles

---

### Angle 2: "Woke Up Unhinged"
**Tone:** Chaotic, ridiculous reasoning, impulsive Julie
**When:** Deep discounts, flash deals, clearance
**Template:**
```
I woke up at 4am and decided [ridiculous reason].
So now [Product] is $[price].
Alice is going to LOSE it when she sees this.
[link]
Don't tell her.
— Julie
```
**Product fit:** Deep discounts, clearance, impulse-buy price points

---

### Angle 3: "Alice Will Find Out"
**Tone:** Ticking clock comedy, conspiratorial
**When:** Mid-range products, time-sensitive offers
**Template:**
```
Alice left for lunch.
I have approximately 47 minutes.
[Product] — [deal details].
Move fast. She checks the spreadsheet at 2pm.
[link]
— Julie (currently hiding from accounting)
```
**Product fit:** Mid-range items, moderate discounts

---

### Angle 4: "The Witch's Thank You"
**Tone:** Genuine gratitude, no selling pressure
**When:** Post-campaign, milestones, community moments. MAX 1x per 2 weeks.
**Template:**
```
No deal. No link. Just... thank you.
[Specific thing the community did or milestone reached]
This coven is everything.
— Julie
```
**Product fit:** NONE. This is pure relationship-building.
**Rule:** Max 1x per 2 weeks. Overuse kills authenticity.

---

### Angle 5: "The Dare"
**Tone:** Bold, challenging, competitive
**When:** Limited stock, polarizing products, statement pieces
**Template:**
```
This bag isn't for everyone.
[Product] — $[price].
If you're brave enough: [link]
(Brenda would never.)
— Julie
```
**Product fit:** Statement pieces, bold designs, limited stock

---

### Angle 6: "Jade's Studio Confession"
**Tone:** Behind-the-scenes, craftsmanship, reverence
**When:** New collections, design reveals, product stories
**Template:**
```
Jade just showed me something.
I wasn't supposed to see it yet but...
[Design detail or product tease]
First look: [link]
— Julie (I'm not sorry)
```
**Product fit:** New products, design-forward pieces, collections

---

### Angle 7: "The Brenda Report"
**Tone:** Polarization comedy, light mockery
**When:** When email that day was Brenda-themed, contrast moments
**Template:**
```
Update: Brenda bought a beige crossbody today.
For $12.99.
At Target.
Meanwhile: [Product] — [deal]. [link]
Choose your darkness wisely.
— Julie
```
**Product fit:** Any — works as contrast device
**Rule:** Max 1x/week. Pair with Brenda email days.

---

### Angle 8: "Live From The Chaos"
**Tone:** Breathless, real-time, CAPSLOCK energy
**When:** Active sales, live events, Day 1 launches
**Template:**
```
[Timestamp] UPDATE:
[Product] just sold out in [time].
Alice is SCREAMING.
[What's still available]: [link]
This is NOT a drill.
— Julie
```
**Product fit:** Sale items, trending products, limited stock

---

### Angle 9: "The Receipt"
**Tone:** Ultra-short, punchy, social proof
**When:** Between longer SMS, palate cleanser, high-volume days
**Template:**
```
"[Customer quote — raw, with typos/emoji]"
— [Name], [City]
She got the [Product]: [link]
```
**Chars:** Under 160. The shortest angle.
**Product fit:** Any — driven by available customer quote

---

### Angle 10: "Coven Intel"
**Tone:** Insider access, pre-launch, conspiratorial
**When:** Pre-launch, early access windows, subscriber-only deals
**Template:**
```
Sending this before we post it anywhere.
[Product/collection] drops [when].
You're seeing it first because... coven.
Early access: [link]
— Julie
```
**Product fit:** New launches, pre-sales, early access

| W2 | Angle 10 (Intel) | Angle 9 (Receipt) | Angle 5 (Dare) | Angle 8 (Chaos) |
| W3 | Angle 2 (Unhinged) | Angle 7 (Brenda) | Angle 1 (Hid One) | Angle 8 (Chaos) |
| W4 | Angle 4 (Thank You) | Angle 6 (Jade) | Angle 10 (Intel) | — |

**Rules:**
- Never same angle twice in one week
- Angle 4 (Thank You) max 1x per 2 weeks
- Angle 7 (Brenda) max 1x per week
- Angle 8 (Chaos) only during active sales
- Angle 9 (Receipt) = great filler between heavy SMS days

## ANGLE × SEGMENT PAIRING

| Segment | Best Angles | Avoid |
|---------|-------------|-------|
| REG (registered) | 8, 1, 3, 2 | 4, 10 (they already opted in) |
| HIGH (engaged) | 1, 4, 6, 10, 5 | 8 (save chaos for REG) |
| MASS (full list) | 9, 5, 7, 2 | 1, 4 (too personal for cold) |
