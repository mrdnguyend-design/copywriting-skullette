# MODULE 4: PROMOTION CAMPAIGN ARCHITECTURE — Multi-Day Sales

## 4.1 — G.A.M.E.S.H.O.W. FRAMEWORK

> *Retrofitted từ Daniel Throssell's Black Friday MADNESS campaign — tạo ra nhiều revenue trong 1 tuần hơn cả năm lương kỹ sư.*

### 8 Nguyên tắc — với áp dụng thực chiến

---

### G — Genuine Bargain

> **"All my best offers have GENUINELY made me feel like I was being an idiot for charging so little."**

| ❌ Fake Scarcity | ✅ Genuine Bargain |
|---|---|
| "Up to 50% off selected items" | "$49.95 wallets for $9.95. I might be an idiot." |
| Inflated original price | Deal tốt đến mức FOUNDER cảm thấy khó chịu |
| "Limited time!" (nhưng sale mỗi tuần) | "I need to apologize to that bag personally" |

**Test:** Bạn có feel uncomfortable với mức giá sale không? Nếu KHÔNG → deal chưa đủ tốt.

**Payoff:** Genuine bargain trains customers to:
1. Open emails eagerly (every email could be a deal)
2. Associate buying with happy emotions
3. Buy FAST (because they know it's real)

---

### A — Don't Be Ashamed of Selling

> **"GOOD SELLING IS ENTERTAINING. Consumers don't want to be virtue-signalled to. THEY WANT TO BE SOOOOOLD."**

| ❌ Ashamed | ✅ Shameless |
|---|---|
| "I know, another sale email... sorry!" | "I just did something stupid. Let me tell you." |
| "I don't usually do this but..." | "600 orders. When they're gone, gone." |
| Self-deprecating about selling | Self-deprecating about PRICES (look what I'm giving away) |

**Mindset shift:** Selling isn't interrupting. Selling IS the entertainment.

---

### M — Be (A Little) Mad

> **"If people perceive you as crazy, they believe the deal IS an irrationally good bargain."**

**The Narrative Formula:**
```
"I'm doing this because I'm slightly insane"
    → Reader believes the deal is GENUINELY crazy
    → Urgency increases (crazy person might change their mind)
```

**Áp dụng Skullette:**
- Julie's accountant emailing just "Julie." (no body text)
- "Alice looked at the prices and said '...oh. oh no.'"
- "I woke up at 6 AM and made a decision my accountant will hear about on Monday"

---

### E — Make It An Event

> **Ba elements bắt buộc: 1) A NAME. 2) A clearly defined START/END. 3) Rarely repeated.**

| Element | Ví dụ |
|---------|-------|
| **Name** | SKULL-A-PALOOZA, The Final 300, SECRET SANTA, Black Friday Madness |
| **Start/End** | "Mar 17-19, 72 hours. 10:00 AM ET launch." |
| **Rarity** | "I'm not doing this twice." |

**Naming Tips:**
- Tên phải FUN, memorable, slightly absurd
- Lặp lại tên trong MỌI email/SMS suốt chiến dịch
- Tên = personality × occasion ("SKULL-A-PALOOZA" = skulls + festival + palooza energy)

---

### S — Lean On Scarcity

> **Quantity scarcity > Time scarcity**

| Scarcity Type | Sức mạnh | Ví dụ |
|---|---|---|
| **Time** | ⭐⭐⭐ | "Sale ends in 24 hours" |
| **Quantity** | ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ | "600 orders. When they're gone, prices go back." |
| **Both** | ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ | "600 orders across 72 hours. 55 spots left." |

**Rules:**
1. Scarcity phải CỤ THỂ — "50 bags" > "limited stock"
2. Update scarcity numbers REAL-TIME trong SMS — "we're at 545. 55 spots remain."
3. Report sold-out items — "Bloodraven Mini Satchel? ☠️ gone." → loss aversion

---

### H — Hard to Predict

> **"If people don't know what's coming next, they keep tuning in."**

**5 chiều Unpredictability:**

| Dimension | Tạo biến đổi bằng cách... |
|-----------|---------------------------|
| **Offer types** | Mix bags, wallets, bundles, new drops |
| **Price points** | $9.95 → $29.95 → $49.95, không pattern |
| **Availability** | Một số capped, một số open |
| **Timing** | Reshuffles mỗi 3 giờ, surprise drops |
| **Content style** | Email #1 = story, #2 = announcement, #3 = behind-scenes |

**Skullette Application:**
- Price shuffles mỗi 3 giờ (Friday 13th)
- New product categories drop mỗi ngày (SKULL-A-PALOOZA: Day 1 wallets → Day 2 bags → Day 3 satchels + circle bags)
- No hints about upcoming drops → must stay tuned

---

### O — Stay Offer Focused

> **"If you have an unbelievably SEXY OFFER, people will be too busy WANTING it to care how hard you're selling."**

**The Test:**
- Bạn có thể email list 1 tuần KHÔNG CÓ "value" nào ngoài offers... và list vẫn LOVE?
- Nếu CÓ → offer đủ mạnh. Keep selling.
- Nếu KHÔNG → offer chưa đủ sexy. Fix offer trước, không phải fix copy.

---

### W — Wing It!

> **"Campaigns ALWAYS work better when you run them LIVE."**

**Tại sao live > pre-written?**

1. **Adapt to reality** — real order counts, real sold-out items, real customer reactions
2. **Serendipity** — unexpected record-breaking orders → best content
3. **Authenticity** — reader FEELS the energy is real vì nó IS real

**Pre-written vs Live balance:**

| Phase | Pre-written? | Live? |
|-------|-------------|-------|
| Teaser emails | ✅ Viết trước | ❌ |
| Warmup emails | ✅ Viết trước | ❌ |
| Sale Day EMAIL | ✅ Template + fill | Partially |
| Sale Day SMS | Template sẵn, fill real data | ✅ LIVE |

## 4.2 — 3-PHASE CAMPAIGN TIMELINE

### Pha 1: TEASER (5-7 ngày trước sale)

**Mục tiêu:** Tạo curiosity → push keyword opt-in → build anticipation

| Ngày | Email | SMS |
|------|-------|-----|
| D-7 | E1: Announcement — "Something's coming" | SM1: Quick teaser (Mass) |
| D-6 | E2: Price shock — "Is $9.95 real?" | — |
| D-5 | E3: Social proof / testimonials | SH1: Story teaser (High Engage) |
| D-4 | E4: Daily + strategy reveal | — |
| D-3 | E5: Last Call to Register | SM2: Urgency (Mass) |
| D-2 | — | SH2: Last call (High Engage) |
| D-1 | E6: Final teaser | SM3: "Tomorrow. Be ready." (Mass) |

**Content Mechanics:**
- Mỗi teaser email = 1 "reveal" mới (price, products, access, mechanic)
- Build FOMO qua progression: "500 witches registered... 800... 1000..."
- KHÔNG reveal tất cả — mỗi email chỉ 1 puzzle piece

---

### Pha 2: WARMUP (2-3 ngày trước sale — Registered only)

**Mục tiêu:** Registered segment cảm thấy EXCLUSIVE + prepared

| Ngày | Email (Registered) | SMS (Registered) |
|------|-------------------|------------------|
| D-3 | WE1: Insider preview — what's dropping | WS1: "You're on the list" |
| D-2 | WE2: Browse prep — "2 days out" | WS2: Warehouse preview |
| D-1 | WE3: Game plan — Alice/Jamie prep | WS3: "Tomorrow 10AM. Be ready." |

**Key Principle:** Warmup emails = behind-the-scenes CHARACTER MOMENTS

- Alice's 7-phase spreadsheet
- Jamie going to bed at 7pm ("I AM A PACKING ATHLETE")
- Jennifer đã pick out bag cụ thể → "Do YOU have a plan?"

---

### Pha 3: SALE DAY (1-3 ngày)

**Mục tiêu:** Convert. Real-time energy. Community celebration.

**Email Structure:**

| Ngày | Email Registered | Email Mass |
|------|-----------------|-----------|
| Day 1 | SD-E1: "IT'S LIVE — Your Link" | SD-E2: Chaos FOMO |
| Day 2 | SD-E3: Day 2 update | SD-E4: Day 2 FOMO |
| Day 3 | SD-E5: Final Day | SD-E6: "Closing Tonight" |

**SMS Structure (Registered — xem Module 3 Section 3.5):**
- 6-8 SMS/ngày, cách nhau 1-3 giờ
- Mix: Launch → Chaos → Character → Scoreboard → Countdown → Celebration

### Post-Sale (Day +1)

| Content | Mục đích |
|---------|---------|
| PS-E1: Recap email | Celebrate community. NO selling. |
| Thank you SMS (optional) | Short, emotional, real |

## 4.3 — GAMIFICATION MECHANICS

### 5 mechanics đã proven effective

| Mechanic | Cách hoạt động | Campaign |
|----------|----------------|----------|
| **Price Shuffles** | Giá thay đổi mỗi 3 giờ → tạo "treasure hunt" | Friday the 13th |
| **Daily Drops** | Sản phẩm mới mỗi ngày → lý do quay lại | SKULL-A-PALOOZA |
| **Order Cap** | 600 orders = done. Rõ ràng, real. | SKULL-A-PALOOZA |
| **Leaderboard** | Report biggest orders → inspire competition | Sale Day SMS |
| **Keyword Exclusivity** | Text keyword = access. No keyword = miss out. | All campaigns |

### Gamification Design Rule

> **Mỗi mechanic phải tạo ra 1 lý do để QUAY LẠI CHECK.**

- Price shuffle → "Bag bạn muốn có thể rẻ hơn round sau"
- Daily drops → "Tomorrow = new products"
- Leaderboard → "Someone just broke the record — can you beat her?"

## 4.4 — CAMPAIGN SIZING GUIDE

### From Simple → Complex

| Level | Duration | Total Content | Examples |
|-------|----------|--------------|---------|
| **Flash Sale** | 1 day | 3-4 pieces | Labor Day, Flash Sale, Prime Day |
| **Weekend Special** | 2 days | 6-8 pieces | Weekend Specials, BOGO |
| **Mid-Tier Event** | 2 days + 1 week teaser | 15-18 pieces | Friday the 13th, Dark Deal Day |
| **Major Event** | 3 days + 1 week teaser | 30-35 pieces | SKULL-A-PALOOZA |
| **Mega Event** | 7 days + 1 week teaser | 40+ pieces | SECRET SANTA, Black Friday Madness |

### Production Time Estimate

| Level | Writing Time | HTML/Formatting | Total |
|-------|-------------|----------------|-------|
| Flash Sale | 4-6 hours | 2 hours | ~1 day |
| Weekend Special | 8-12 hours | 4 hours | ~2 days |
| Mid-Tier | 16-24 hours | 6 hours | ~3-4 days |
| Major | 24-40 hours | 8 hours | ~5 days |
| Mega | 40-60 hours | 12 hours | ~7-10 days |
