## 3. THE "HOT DOG vs. BROCCOLI" AUDIT CHECKLIST
*Run every piece of content through this checklist before publishing.*

**Content Title/Subject:** ___________________

**Pass/Fail Criteria:**
- [ ] **Is the Hook Boring?** (If it sounds like a textbook -> **FAIL**. If it sounds like a tabloid/conversation -> **PASS**).
- [ ] **Is the "Broccoli" Hidden?** Is the teaching wrapped inside a story, or is it naked? (Naked teaching = **FAIL**).
- [ ] **Is there a Personality Flaw?** Did you reveal a weakness, an opinion, or a quirk? (Too polished = **FAIL**).
- [ ] **Is there a Villain?** Is there a common enemy (bad gurus, government, mindset) we are fighting against?
- [ ] **Is the CTA Unapologetic?** Did you ask for the sale directly, or did you apologize for selling? (Apology = **FAIL**).

> **Score:** If you checked fewer than 4 boxes, rewrite the intro.

## 4. THE TROLL MONETIZATION TEMPLATE
*How to turn hate mail or negative comments into profit (The Upstairs Troll).*

**The Source Material:** `[Paste the nasty comment/email here]`

**The Analysis:**
1.  **Is it irrational?** (Yes/No)
2.  **Does it represent a common objection?** (Yes/No)

**The Response Strategy (Do NOT reply to them directly. Reply to your list):**
* **Headline:** "Someone is angry..." or "Fan mail from the asylum"
* **The Setup:** "Look what this snowflake sent me today..."
* **The Takedown:** Dismantle their logic to show why they are poor/unsuccessful/wrong.
* **The Lesson:** "Don't be like [Name]. If you want to actually succeed, do [Product]."
* **The Moral:** "If this offends you, please unsubscribe. If you want results, click here."
