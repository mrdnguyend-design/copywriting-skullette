5. **Emotional arc hoàn hảo** — vulnerability (childhood doubt) → revelation (Sonja's gift) → pride (mother's declaration) → action (buy/forward)
6. **P.S. addressing Sonja directly** — tạo cảm giác email là thật, không phải marketing

**Key learnings → UPDATE WORKFLOW:**
